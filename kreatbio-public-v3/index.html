KodaPure

DNA extraction kit for routine research work.

KodaPure is a magnetic-bead DNA extraction kit specialized in bacteria, animal tissue, soil/dirty samples.

  • Pure bacterial culture, soil, animal tissue
  • Column-free workflow with representative timing and specifications
  • Quick protocol and SDS available

KodaGeno

Sequencing analysis training for classes, programs, and learners.

KodaGeno is a guided sequencing analysis training product for courses, programs, and individual learners.

  • Bacterial WGS and amplicon lessons are live now, with RNA-seq coming soon.
  • Built for universities, training programs, and self-study
  • Try out the free tutorials!
