03DNA Extraction & Sequencing

DNA extraction and sequencing support for research workflows

DNA extraction, quantification and NGS sequencing support for teams that need reliable genomics output without building the full workflow internally.

KodaXtract

DNA Extraction Service

Scope
DNA extraction from submitted biological samples for downstream molecular workflows, including PCR, qPCR, sequencing and further analysis.
Typical Applications
Suitable for research teams that require extracted DNA without managing the full extraction workflow internally, including batch sample submissions, project volume spikes and pre-sequencing preparation.
Deliverables
Extracted DNA, sample-level handover information and workflow notes where relevant.

KodaSeq

NGS Sequencing

Scope
NGS sequencing for teams that need sequencing output without buying instruments, setting up library preparation or maintaining a full sequencing workflow internally.
Services Included
  • Whole-genome sequencing
  • Shotgun metagenomics for microbiome analysis
  • Bacterial 16S rRNA sequencing
  • Fungal ITS sequencing
Why Long Reads
ONT chemistry and basecalling accuracy have improved over recent years. Long reads can cover longer targets such as full-length 16S, which can improve taxonomic resolution and support species-level identification where short-read marker regions may be less specific.
Why Illumina Short Reads
Illumina short-read sequencing offers high per-base accuracy, scalable throughput and established analysis workflows for whole-genome sequencing, shotgun metagenomics, amplicon profiling and variant detection.
Deliverables
Sequencing data files, run-level quality information and basic sequencing summary statistics. Optional downstream bioinformatics analysis is available through KodaAly.
04DNA Quantification & Extraction Kit

Sample preparation tools for downstream molecular analysis

Supporting workflow readiness through DNA quantification and in-house extraction capability.

KodaQuant

DNA Quantification Service

Scope
DNA concentration measurement from submitted extracted DNA samples to support downstream workflow planning.
Typical Applications
Used before PCR, qPCR, sequencing, library preparation or other workflows where DNA concentration needs to be confirmed before proceeding.
Deliverables
Sample-level DNA concentration results and a clear readout to support downstream experimental decisions.

KodaPure

DNA Extraction Kit

Scope
Magnetic bead-based DNA extraction kit for laboratories that prefer to conduct sample preparation in-house.
Typical Applications
Suitable for teaching laboratories, research cores and teams processing recurring sample batches where internal workflow control is preferred.
Deliverables
DNA extraction kit and supporting workflow materials for in-house sample preparation before PCR, qPCR, sequencing and related applications.
05Bioinformatics & Training

Bioinformatics support from sequencing data to practical capability building

Combining analysis support and guided learning to help teams interpret data and build internal capability.

KodaAly

Customized Bioinformatics Analysis

Scope
Customized bioinformatics analysis that converts sequencing data into organized outputs for review, reporting, publication support or follow-on research decisions.
Typical Applications
Supports bacterial whole-genome sequencing analysis, fungal whole-genome sequencing analysis, bacterial and fungal amplicon analysis, and customized workflows for specific research questions.
Deliverables
Quality summaries, processed analysis outputs, tables, visualizations, annotation or taxonomic outputs where relevant, and concise interpretation notes.

KodaGeno

Bioinformatics Training Platform

Scope
Structured bioinformatics training platform that helps institutions deliver practical sequencing analysis training through guided lessons, datasets and exercises.
Typical Applications
Suitable for course delivery, departmental training, research team upskilling and guided student learning where practical analysis exposure is required.
Deliverables
Structured learning modules, hands-on datasets and exercises, learner progress visibility and instructor-friendly teaching support. Current content includes bacterial whole-genome sequencing lessons, amplicon analysis lessons, and RNA-seq modules under development.
